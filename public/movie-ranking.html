Best Movies According to Bob

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

The Dark Knight

One of my favourite superhero movies, it's really unique and I really don't need to say much about Heath Ledger's joker and also Christian Bale's Batman potrayal.

Hangover

This movie is just hilarious, I have watched it multiple times and it never gets old. The plot is really funny and the characters are really well developed.

Saving Private Ryan

This movie is a masterpiece, the war scenes are really well done and the story is really touching. Tom Hanks did an amazing job in this movie.
